# Biography

Kazuaki Oyama was born in Chiba, Japan in 2004. He learned basic electrical expertises at Tokyo Metropolitan College of Industrial Technology (TMCIT). After that he entered department of physics, Tokyo Metropolitan University (TMU). He currently interested in the fundamental physics for next-generation spin, optical or magnetic devices using first principle calculations.
